THERE IS ALWAYS HOPE!!

When the things go wrong as they sometimes must!

When the circumstances crush every effort to dust

When you lose no matter how hard you try

When you are alone and it is so easy to cry

You must realise that in any design

Not every thread is meant to shine

So give yourself another chance

Rise again and change your stance

As the thread would certainly be bright one day

When you will win and would proudly say

"Aim the target and don't limit your scope

As my dear friend there is always hope!!"
