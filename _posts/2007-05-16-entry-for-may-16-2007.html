A DAY MAY COME...
A day may come when i would quite
when i would lament over my past mistakes,
but it is not this day.
A day when i would cry out for help,
when i would balk at the obstacles ahead,
but it is not this day.
A day when i would kneel infront of problems,
and would say no to stepping forward,
but it is not this day.
A day when i would choose to capitulate rather than contemplate,
when i would let all possibilities to fret away,
but it is not this day.
A day when darkness would prevail ,
and i would succumb to fear and pressure,
but it is not this day!
THIS DAY I FIGHT!!
