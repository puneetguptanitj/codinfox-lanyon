A STRANGER IN DISGUISE

Few days back I happened to meet a stranger. He stayed with me for a while and I soon realised that he was rather a true friend. Not that he did something great for me..but how I felt being with him. It was quite a different and cathartic experience. I later came to know that he had tried to come to me lot many times before as well only I didnot listen. I probably was too busy with my life.
He didnot talk much but preferred to listen most of the times. It seemed as if he could listen to all my problems without blinking even once! I could see deeper inside myself and could understand myself better when he was around. The best part was that I could easily go to him anytime and he was always eager to be with me.
I felt so much better after talking to him. The other day when we were together doorbell rang and I didn't like that at all... still as I went to open the door my friend said "I would have to go now". I said "Oh... ok ...see you soon". He said don't worry I will be around! .."but I don't even know your name!!" I shouted
.....He smiled and said "...People call me Solitude!!!"
