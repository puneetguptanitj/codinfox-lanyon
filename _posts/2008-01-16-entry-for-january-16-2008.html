Why should one try to be happy !

Heat is the degree of hotness or coldness of a body. Similarly happiness also has some degree. It is a measurable phenomenon, you can compare whether a person is happier or sadder than someone else. Thus it is something relative and thus must be some entity (need not be tangible) in the space time domain. Why do people say that one should try to be ever full of joy and happiness. Now during smiling we use lesser muscles than while we are angry or sad thus in a way being sad disspiates energy while being happy conserves it. Thus happy state is a higher energy state. So is meditation (though the energy bar here is lot higher). A non-meditator vibrates at 300 cycles per second while a meditated mind vibrates at 200 thousand cycles per second.
