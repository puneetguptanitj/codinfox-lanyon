WHEN PEOPLE SAY...

When people say stop

but you feel like moving on

When people say you will lose

but you know that you'll love to try

when people say "come on don't be silly"

but you know that you were serious

when people say that you are too young

but you know it doesn't matter

when people laugh at you

but you know to err is human

when people say you know so less

but you know that you are learning

when people say there is no other way

but somehow you are not convinced

when people say your are lost

but you know where you are going

when people say your are good for nothing

but you know your own worth

...perhaps you should know one more thing

DON'T GIVE A DAMN TO WHAT THEY SAY!!!!!
