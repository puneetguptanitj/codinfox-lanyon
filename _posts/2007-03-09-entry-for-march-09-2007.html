ITS STRENGTH OF THE WINGS THAT WE SEEK

Now the road ahead is to be walked alone,
There is no time to weep or morn,
Failures and successes are to be kept aside
Walk with strength as what matters is the ride

Lots of people have lost and won
Tasks have been finished and some are still left undone
Its not the pace of walk thats needed to be high
whats needed is that you face detours but still donot return a sigh,

Walking alone would never be easy,
It would be tougher when path would be hazy,
You would miss the aegis that had protected you for so long,
but i guess its time to unbelong,

It doesn't mean you are leaving your roots,
rather, you are giving them strength to nourish better fruits,
Chrysalis is hard because it is not meant to be weak,
as its not an easier path but strength of wings that we seek,
